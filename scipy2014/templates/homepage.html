Attend

The annual SciPy Conference allows participants from academic, commercial, and governmental organizations to showcase their latest Scientific Python projects, learn from skilled users and developers, and collaborate on code development.

We look forward to a very exciting conference and hope to see you at the 2014 conference.

Look Who's Talkin'

The speakers have been chosen and are busy creating yet another banner year for the SciPy conference

The list of over 100 speakers can be found at on the Talks/Posters page. This year we have new minisymposium topics including Engineering with an outstanding showing from our two topic tracks: Education and Geospatial computing.

This year our tutorials grows to four concurrent tracks to make for a stellar list of Accepted Tutorials. Adding a topics track this year, we hope to give participants a view into computing in new areas of science.

Plan

The 2014 conference will consist of two days of tutorials followed by three days of presentations, and concludes with two days of developer sprints on projects of interest to the attendees.
